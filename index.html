What is Flat Fee MLS?

Before diving into the benefits, let's clarify: The Multiple Listing Service (MLS) is the primary real estate database used by agents to find properties for their buyers. However, only licensed realtors can list properties directly on the MLS. Flat fee MLS services connect sellers with licensed agents who will list homes on the MLS for a one-time flat fee, instead of a traditional percentage commission.

This means you get all the advantages of MLS exposure while avoiding expensive agent commissions.
